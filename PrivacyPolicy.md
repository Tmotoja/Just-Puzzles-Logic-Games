Privacy Policy for Just Puzzles: Logic Games

Last updated: July 2026

Thank you for playing Just Puzzles: Logic Games. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our mobile application ("Just Puzzles: Logic Games").

1. Information We Collect (Directly)
Just 2048 is built as a commercial app. The developer does not directly collect, store, or share any personal data, account information, or gameplay details on any external servers. Your game progress, high scores, statistics, and unlocked achievements are saved locally on your device.

2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services
To support the game, Just Puzzles: Logic Games integrates third-party advertising services provided by Google AdMob. If you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, these third-party networks may collect and use data to display personalized advertisements.

The categories of data processed by Google AdMob include:
• Identifiers: Device ID (such as the Advertising Identifier / IDFA).
• Usage Data: Information regarding your interaction with ads within the app (e.g., ad views, clicks).
• Location Data: Coarse/approximate location derived from your IP address to show geographically relevant ads.
• Diagnostics: Anonymous crash logs and performance metrics to help keep the app stable.

You can learn more about how Google handles your data by visiting Google's Privacy & Terms: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and Opt-Out
On iOS devices, you can choose whether to allow Just 2048 to track your activity across other apps and websites for advertising purposes. If you select "Ask App not to Track", Google AdMob will still display ads, but they will be non-personalized (contextual only) and will not use your IDFA.

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device settings under: Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Tracking.

4. Children's Privacy
Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to third-party ad networks through this app, please contact us so we can address the situation.
